#StayAtHome

Sharing your stories from across the USC community and southern California



This website provides a small outlet for sharing your stories - big and small. Our lived experiences bring us together. Share something about how you have been impacted during the past few days and now weeks. You can write about anything. Share a favorite place from your hometown and what it means to you during this time. Let us know how you are staying connected with friends and family. What are you doing to stay healthy and positive? You can share more than one story.
